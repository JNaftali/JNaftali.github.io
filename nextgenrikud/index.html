Welcome to NextGen Rikud!

Mission:
Foster the growth of Israeli dance in the DC metropolitan area by increasing the child and young adult (next generation) involvement in Israeli dance.

Main Goal:
A website (and FB group) that consolidates all information on events for kids and young adults residing in the DC metro area including: sessions, dance festivals, marathons, performance groups, and special events. We may include events in Philly and NY as well. Links to group/event websites/FB pages will be provided, if available.

Possible Side Effort:
A program that can host Israeli dance special events geared for kids and young adults as requested/needed
